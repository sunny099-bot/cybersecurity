Enterprise Hardware Categories

End-to-end hardware procurement, deployment, and management — from network infrastructure to end-user devices, all backed by expert support and manufacturer warranties.

Network Switches

Enterprise Network Switch Solutions

Deploy high-performance managed switches for campus, data center, and branch networks — from access layer to core, with PoE, stacking, 10G/25G/40G/100G uplinks, and advanced Layer 3 features.

Access & Distribution Layer

Layer 2/3 access switches with PoE+, VLANs, port security, and QoS for connecting endpoints, IP phones, and wireless access points.

  • 24/48-Port Gigabit & Multi-Gig
  • PoE+ / PoE++ (60W/90W per port)
  • VLAN, STP, LACP, 802.1X
  • Stacking & Ring Redundancy

Core & Data Center Switches

High-throughput Layer 3 switches with 10G/25G/40G/100G ports for data center spine-leaf, campus core, and high-density aggregation.

  • 10G/25G/40G/100G Interfaces
  • OSPF, BGP, VRF, MPLS
  • VXLAN & EVPN Support
  • Low-Latency & High-Density

Network Security Features

Port security, DHCP snooping, dynamic ARP inspection, IP source guard, and ACLs to protect against Layer 2/3 threats at the network edge.

Network Management

Centralized management with CLI, web GUI, SNMP, and cloud controllers — real-time monitoring, firmware management, and configuration backup.

Power over Ethernet

Power IP phones, cameras, APs, and IoT devices directly from the switch — PoE+ (30W), PoE++ (60W/90W), and intelligent power budgeting.

Wireless Access Points

Enterprise Access Point Solutions

Design and deploy enterprise Wi-Fi networks with high-density access points — Wi-Fi 6/6E/7, seamless roaming, centralized management, and AI-driven RF optimization for flawless wireless experience.

Wi-Fi 6 / 6E / 7 Access Points

Next-generation APs with OFDMA, MU-MIMO, 160MHz channels, and tri-band operation for ultra-high density and ultra-low latency wireless connectivity.

  • Wi-Fi 6E (6GHz Band)
  • 2x2 / 4x4 / 8x8 MIMO
  • Multi-Gig Ethernet Uplinks
  • Indoor & Outdoor Models

AI-Driven Wireless Management

Cloud or on-premises controllers with AI-powered RF optimization, automatic channel selection, power adjustment, and predictive troubleshooting.

  • AI RF Optimization
  • Seamless 802.11r/k/v Roaming
  • Heatmap & predictive planning
  • Real-time client analytics

High-Density Deployment

Stadiums, convention centers, campuses — engineered for thousands of simultaneous clients per AP with band steering and load balancing.

Wireless Security

WPA3-Enterprise, 802.1X, dynamic VLAN assignment, rogue AP detection, wireless IDS/IPS, and zero-trust onboarding for guests and IoT.

Location & Analytics

Indoor location tracking, wayfinding, proximity marketing, and presence analytics using built-in BLE and Wi-Fi triangulation capabilities.

Enterprise Servers

High-Performance Server Infrastructure

Rack servers, tower servers, and blade systems from leading OEMs — configured for virtualization, database, AI/ML, and mission-critical workloads with redundant power, cooling, and remote management.

Rack & Tower Servers

1U/2U/4U rack servers and tower form factors — Intel Xeon and AMD EPYC processors, up to 8TB RAM, NVMe SSD storage, and PCIe 5.0 expansion.

  • Intel Xeon Scalable / AMD EPYC
  • Up to 8TB DDR5 RAM
  • NVMe, SSD, HDD Storage Options
  • Redundant PSU (1+1 / N+1)

Blade & HCI Systems

High-density blade chassis and hyper-converged infrastructure — maximize compute density with shared storage, networking, and simplified management.

  • Blade Chassis (8-16 Blades)
  • Hyper-Converged Appliances
  • Integrated 10/25Gb Networking
  • Centralized Chassis Management

GPU Servers (AI/ML)

NVIDIA A100/H100 GPU servers for AI training, inference, and HPC workloads — with NVLink, InfiniBand, and high-bandwidth GPU interconnect.

Storage Servers

High-capacity storage servers with JBOD expansion, RAID controllers, and all-flash or hybrid configurations for file, block, and object storage.

iDRAC / iLO Management

Out-of-band management with Dell iDRAC, HPE iLO, or Lenovo XClarity — remote KVM, virtual media, firmware updates, and hardware monitoring.

Network Attached Storage

Enterprise NAS Storage Solutions

Deploy reliable NAS storage for file sharing, backup targets, VM datastores, and media workflows — with RAID protection, snapshot technology, scale-out clustering, and hybrid cloud sync.

SMB & Mid-Range NAS

2-bay to 12-bay desktop and rackmount NAS for small offices and branch locations — file sharing, backup, surveillance, and media streaming.

  • 2 to 12-Bay Configurations
  • RAID 0/1/5/6/10 + SHR
  • 10GbE & 2.5GbE Options
  • SSD Caching Acceleration

Enterprise & Scale-Out NAS

High-capacity rack NAS and scale-out clustering for enterprise — petabyte-scale storage with high availability, deduplication, and tiering.

  • 24 to 120+ Bay Systems
  • Scale-Out Clustering
  • Inline Dedup & Compression
  • 25/40/100GbE Networking

Surveillance & NVR Storage

Dedicated surveillance NAS with optimized write performance for continuous camera recording — supports 50 to 500+ IP cameras with AI-powered video analytics integration, event-based recording, and retention policy management.

Hybrid Cloud & Sync

Sync on-premises NAS data to public cloud (AWS S3, Azure Blob, Google Cloud) with encryption, versioning, and selective sync. Enable hybrid cloud workflows, cloud backup targets, and multi-site replication for disaster recovery.

Snapshots & Data Protection

Instant point-in-time snapshots with near-zero RPO — take up to 1,024 snapshots per volume with scheduled policies, remote replication to DR sites, and self-service file-level recovery from any snapshot without IT intervention.
