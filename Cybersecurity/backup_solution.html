Our Solutions

Enterprise Backup Solutions

Industry-leading backup platforms deployed, configured, and managed by our certified engineers — ensuring your data is always protected and instantly recoverable.

Acronis Cyber Protect

Unified Acronis Backup & Cyber Protection

Go beyond traditional backup with Acronis Cyber Protect — combining backup, disaster recovery, anti-malware, and security in a single integrated platform. Protect physical, virtual, cloud, and mobile workloads with AI-powered ransomware defense.

AI-Powered Ransomware Protection

Acronis uses machine learning to detect and block ransomware attacks in real-time — stopping encryption before it spreads and automatically rolling back affected files.

  • Real-Time Ransomware Detection
  • Automatic File Rollback
  • Behavioral Analysis Engine
  • Crypto-Blocker Technology

Universal Backup & Cloud Storage

Back up any workload to any location — on-premises, Acronis Cloud, or any public cloud. Supports full, incremental, and differential backup with deduplication and compression.

  • Physical & Virtual Machines
  • Cloud & Hybrid Deployments
  • Global Deduplication
  • AES-256 Encryption

Bare-Metal Recovery

Restore entire systems to dissimilar hardware or virtual environments with bootable media and universal restore capabilities for fast disaster recovery.

Mobile & Endpoint Backup

Protect iOS and Android devices — back up contacts, photos, messages, and app data to Acronis Cloud with remote wipe and device tracking capabilities.

SaaS Backup (M365 & G-Workspace)

Back up Microsoft 365 and Google Workspace data — emails, drives, contacts, and calendars — with granular restore and point-in-time recovery.

Centralized Management

Manage all backup policies, security settings, and recovery operations from a single web-based console with role-based access and multi-tenant support.

Disaster Recovery Orchestrator

Automated DR runbooks with failover testing, RTO/RPO monitoring, and one-click site recovery for business continuity planning and compliance.

Anti-Malware & URL Filtering

Integrated next-gen antivirus, web filtering, and patch management — protect endpoints from malware, phishing, and vulnerabilities alongside backup operations.

Veeam Availability Suite

Enterprise Veeam Backup & Replication

Achieve ultimate data availability with Veeam — the industry standard for VM backup, cloud-native protection, and instant recovery. From on-premises vSphere and Hyper-V to AWS, Azure, and Kubernetes workloads.

Instant VM Recovery

Recover an entire VM in minutes — not hours. Veeam's Instant VM Recovery starts the VM directly from the backup file while restoring in the background for zero-downtime recovery.

  • Recovery in Under 15 Minutes
  • Run Directly from Backup
  • Background Full Restore
  • Zero RPO for Critical VMs

Replication & Failover

Create replica VMs with near-synchronous replication for RPOs as low as seconds. Automated failover with planned and unplanned switchover for seamless DR.

  • Near-Sync Replication
  • Automated Failover & Failback
  • DR Testing Without Impact
  • Multi-Site Replication Chains

VM Backup (vSphere & Hyper-V)

Image-level backup for VMware vSphere and Microsoft Hyper-V with Changed Block Tracking (CBT), synthetic fulls, and forever-forward incremental backups.

Cloud-Native Protection

Back up and restore AWS EC2, RDS, EFS, and Azure VMs, blobs, SQL — with native snapshots, incremental backups, and cross-region DR replication.

NAS & Unstructured Data

Protect file servers and NAS shares with policy-based backup, fast recovery of individual files, and scale-out backup repository for petabyte-scale storage.

Explorers & Granular Recovery

Restore individual files, folders, application items (Exchange mails, SharePoint docs, SQL tables, AD objects) directly from any backup without full VM restore.

Veeam Cloud Connect

Send encrypted backups to our Veeam Cloud Connect infrastructure — off-site DR without building a secondary site. Fast seed, WAN acceleration, and tenant isolation.

Monitoring & Analytics

Veeam ONE monitoring with real-time dashboards, capacity planning, backup success tracking, SLA compliance reporting, and proactive alerting on issues before they impact.

Immutable Backups & Ransomware Protection

Veeam's immutable backup repositories ensure that once data is written, it cannot be modified, encrypted, or deleted — even by admin accounts. Combined with SureBackup automated verification, every backup is tested for malware contamination before being committed, providing defense-in-depth against ransomware.

Kubernetes & Container Backup

Back up and restore Kubernetes clusters — namespaces, deployments, ConfigMaps, secrets, and persistent volumes — with Veeam Kasten. Protect containerized applications running on any cloud or on-premises K8s distribution with application-aware backup and RBAC integration.
