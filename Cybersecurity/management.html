Our Services

IT Infrastructure Management

Comprehensive IT infrastructure management covering network monitoring, security event intelligence, and complete service desk operations.

Network Monitoring System

Real-Time Network Visibility & Performance Monitoring

Gain complete visibility into your network infrastructure with enterprise-grade monitoring that tracks every device, link, and application in real-time. Detect outages before they impact business and optimize network performance proactively.

Device Discovery & Mapping

Automatically discover and map all network devices, topology, and connections across your entire infrastructure with zero manual effort.

Performance Monitoring

Monitor bandwidth utilization, latency, packet loss, CPU, memory, and interface metrics in real-time with customizable dashboards.

Alerting & Notifications

Intelligent threshold-based alerting with multi-channel notifications — email, SMS, Slack, and webhook integrations for instant response.

Traffic Analysis

Deep packet inspection and NetFlow analysis to understand traffic patterns, identify bottlenecks, and optimize bandwidth allocation.

Automated Remediation

Trigger automated workflows to restart services, failover links, or execute scripts when specific network conditions are detected.

Reporting & Compliance

Generate scheduled and on-demand reports for SLA compliance, capacity planning, uptime tracking, and executive summaries.

SIEM & SOR

Security Intelligence & Log Management

Centralize security event collection, correlation, and analysis with enterprise SIEM. Complement it with Security Operations Response (SOR) capabilities for rapid incident investigation, threat hunting, and automated response orchestration.

Log Collection & Aggregation

Collect logs from 500+ sources — firewalls, servers, endpoints, cloud platforms, applications, and databases into a unified platform.

  • Agent & Agentless Collection
  • Real-Time Log Ingestion
  • Structured & Unstructured Parsing
  • Compressed Long-Term Storage

Correlation & Threat Detection

Apply advanced correlation rules, behavioral analytics, and ML models to detect complex multi-stage attacks across your environment.

  • Rule-Based Correlation Engine
  • UEBA — User & Entity Analytics
  • Threat Intelligence Feeds
  • Anomaly Detection Models

Security Operations Response (SOR)

Incident Investigation

Visual timeline reconstruction of security incidents with full log context, endpoint telemetry, and network flow data for fast root-cause analysis.

SOAR Automation

Automated incident response playbooks that isolate compromised hosts, block IPs, quarantine files, and execute containment actions without manual intervention.

Threat Hunting

Proactively search for hidden threats, lateral movement, and persistent attackers using hypothesis-driven queries and behavioral indicators.

IT Service Desk & Asset Management

Streamlined IT Operations & Complete Asset Visibility

Deliver exceptional IT support with an intelligent service desk platform, while maintaining a comprehensive inventory of every IT asset across your organization — from procurement to decommission.

IT Service Desk

Ticket Management

End-to-end ticket lifecycle management with automatic routing, prioritization, SLA tracking, escalation rules, and resolution workflows.

Multi-Channel Support

Accept requests via email, portal, chat, phone, and self-service. Provide omnichannel support experience for all users across the organization.

Knowledge Base

Build a self-service knowledge base with articles, FAQs, and troubleshooting guides to reduce ticket volume and empower end-users.

IT Asset Management

Asset Discovery & Inventory

Automatically discover and catalog all IT assets — hardware, software, licenses, and cloud resources. Maintain a single source of truth for your entire asset portfolio with real-time accuracy.

Lifecycle Management

Track every asset from procurement through deployment, maintenance, and retirement. Automate warranty tracking, renewal alerts, and decommission workflows to maximize asset value.

SLA & Performance Analytics

Track service desk KPIs including first-response time, resolution time, customer satisfaction (CSAT), and agent productivity. Real-time dashboards and automated SLA breach alerts keep your team accountable and efficient.

Software License & Compliance

Monitor software license usage, track entitlements, identify compliance gaps, and automate license reconciliation. Prevent overspending on unused licenses and avoid audit penalties with continuous compliance tracking.

CMDB Integration

Maintain a Configuration Management Database that maps relationships between assets, services, users, and business processes. Enable impact analysis and change management with accurate dependency mapping.
